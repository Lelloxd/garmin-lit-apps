Key Features

Automatic Detection

Uses the accelerometer to count every swing without pressing anything.

Calories & Activity

Saves the session as a Golf activity on Garmin Connect with calorie calculation.

Customizable Swing Speed

Adjust the algorithm to match your swing tempo, from slow chips to powerful drives.

Save Confirmation

New menu lets you review stats before saving, discarding, or resuming.

Precision Engine

Optimized for modern devices like Forerunner 955 and Fenix 8.

Swing Speed Calibration

To access settings, Swipe Left on touchscreens or hold the Menu Button. Choose the speed that best matches your current club and tempo:

Setting Description
Slow Swing Maximum sensitivity, ideal for chips, soft pitches, or junior golfers.
Moderate Swing High sensitivity for effortless, smooth swings.
Average Swing Standard setting recommended for most recreational players.
High Swing Reduced sensitivity to filter out false positives from powerful movements.
Fast Swing Lowest sensitivity, designed for high-velocity full power swings.

Note: Activities with 0 swings and 0 calories are automatically discarded to keep your history clean.
